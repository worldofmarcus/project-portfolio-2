Memory of Queen Oblivion

Welcome to 'Memory of Queen Oblivion', a game created for Portfolio Project #2 (JavaScript) - Diploma in Full Stack Software Development Diploma at the Code Institute.

Anyhow. I need your help! And I need your help fast! I'm sad to say that the evil queen of Oblivion has taken my precious diamond and all my keys that goes to the castles of my kingdom. She has cast a spell that has trapped the diamond and keys in pairs inside a set of cards (don't ask me why).

The only way you can help me is to find all of the matching cards. When you have matched all cards you have saved me! Press the 'start game'-button to begin your journey. Good luck!
